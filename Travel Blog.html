Discover Sabah

Explore some of the most beautiful and exciting places to visit in Sabah!

Climbing Mount Kinabalu

Experience the breathtaking views from the highest peak in Southeast Asia.

Diving at Sipadan Island

Discover the vibrant marine life and crystal-clear waters of one of the world's best dive sites.

Wildlife Adventure in Taman Sepilok, Kinabatangan

Spot rare wildlife, including the famous proboscis monkeys, in this stunning rainforest.

Relaxing at Kokol Hill

Enjoy the stunning sunset view and the cool breeze at one of Sabah’s best hilltop retreats.

Glamping at Cabana Retreat

Experience luxury camping on the beach with a breathtaking ocean view.

Hiking at Bukit Bongol, Kota belud

Experience the scenic hills of Bukit Bongol with its stunning sunrise and rolling landscapes.

Adventure at Maragang Hill

Enjoy panoramic views of Mount Kinabalu from the summit of Maragang Hill.

Camping at Tegudon Tourism Village

Experience camping by the river with a magnificent view of Mount Kinabalu.

Camping at Mentoki Hideout

Stay in a hidden paradise surrounded by nature, with a magnificent view of Mount Kinabalu.

Hiking at Bukit Botak, Sepanggar

Discover the top hiking trails in Sabah, with a breathtaking sunset and ocean views.
